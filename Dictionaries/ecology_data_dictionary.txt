
PRAIRIE RESTORATION BIODIVERSITY STUDY - DATA DICTIONARY
==========================================================

STUDY OVERVIEW:
Assessment of insect biodiversity in restored prairie sites compared to 
unrestored control grasslands. Sampling conducted across 4 seasonal periods
at 20 sites (10 restored, 10 control).

Goal: Evaluate whether prairie restoration increases native insect biodiversity.

SITE & SAMPLING IDENTIFIERS
----------------------------
observation_id          Unique observation ID (site_season combination)
site_id                 Site identifier (REST_01-10, CTRL_01-10)
habitat_type            Habitat classification ("Restored_Prairie" or "Control_Grassland")
habitat_code            Numeric habitat code (1=Restored, 0=Control)
season                  Sampling period (Spring, Early_Summer, Late_Summer, Fall)

SITE CHARACTERISTICS
--------------------
site_area_ha            Site area in hectares
restoration_age_yrs     Years since restoration began (NA for controls)
distance_to_remnant_km  Distance to nearest natural habitat remnant (km)

BIODIVERSITY METRICS
--------------------
species_richness        Total number of insect species observed
total_abundance         Total individual insects collected
shannon_index           Shannon diversity index (H')
simpson_index           Simpson diversity index (1-D)
evenness_J              Pielou's evenness index (J)

TAXONOMIC COMPOSITION
---------------------
coleoptera_count        Beetles (Coleoptera) abundance
hymenoptera_count       Bees, wasps, ants (Hymenoptera) abundance
diptera_count           Flies (Diptera) abundance
lepidoptera_count       Butterflies, moths (Lepidoptera) abundance
hemiptera_count         True bugs (Hemiptera) abundance
other_orders_count      Other insect orders combined

FUNCTIONAL GROUPS
-----------------
native_species          Number of native species
nonnative_species       Number of non-native species
prop_native             Proportion of species that are native
pollinator_abundance    Abundance of pollinating insects
predator_abundance      Abundance of predatory insects

ENVIRONMENTAL CONDITIONS (at sampling)
--------------------------------------
temp_celsius            Air temperature (°C)
humidity_pct            Relative humidity (%)
wind_speed_kmh          Wind speed (km/h)
vegetation_height_cm    Mean vegetation height (cm)

NOTES:
- Repeated measures design (4 observations per site)
- Consider mixed models with site as random effect
- Seasonal variation expected in all metrics
- Multiple diversity metrics are correlated
