
CARDIOGUARD-X CLINICAL TRIAL - DATA DICTIONARY
================================================

STUDY OVERVIEW:
A randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled Phase 2b trial evaluating 
CardioGuard-X (a novel anti-inflammatory compound) for reducing cardiovascular 
inflammation markers in patients at high cardiovascular risk.

Primary Endpoint: Change in hs-CRP from baseline to Week 12
Secondary Endpoints: IL-6, fibrinogen, blood pressure changes, clinical response rate

VARIABLE DESCRIPTIONS:

IDENTIFIERS & DESIGN
--------------------
patient_id          Patient identifier (PT0001-PT0240)
treatment_group     Treatment assignment ("CardioGuard-X" or "Placebo")
treatment_code      Numeric treatment code (1=CardioGuard-X, 0=Placebo)
site                Study site (Site_A through Site_F)

DEMOGRAPHICS
------------
age                 Age in years at enrollment
sex                 Sex ("Male" or "Female")
bmi                 Body mass index (kg/m²)
ethnicity           Self-reported ethnicity

MEDICAL HISTORY
---------------
diabetes            History of type 2 diabetes (0=No, 1=Yes)
hypertension        History of hypertension (0=No, 1=Yes)
smoking_status      Smoking status ("Never", "Former", "Current")
statin_use          Currently taking statin medication (0=No, 1=Yes)

BASELINE CLINICAL MEASUREMENTS
------------------------------
baseline_sbp        Systolic blood pressure (mmHg)
baseline_dbp        Diastolic blood pressure (mmHg)
baseline_ldl        LDL cholesterol (mg/dL)
baseline_hdl        HDL cholesterol (mg/dL)
baseline_hba1c      Hemoglobin A1c (%)
baseline_creatinine Serum creatinine (mg/dL)
baseline_egfr       Estimated GFR (mL/min/1.73m²)
baseline_hscrp      High-sensitivity C-reactive protein (mg/L)
baseline_il6        Interleukin-6 (pg/mL)
baseline_fibrinogen Fibrinogen (mg/dL)

OUTCOME MEASUREMENTS
--------------------
week4_hscrp         hs-CRP at Week 4 (mg/L)
week12_hscrp        hs-CRP at Week 12 (mg/L) - PRIMARY ENDPOINT
week24_hscrp        hs-CRP at Week 24 (mg/L) - follow-up
week12_il6          IL-6 at Week 12 (pg/mL)
week12_fibrinogen   Fibrinogen at Week 12 (mg/dL)
week12_sbp          Systolic BP at Week 12 (mmHg)
week12_dbp          Diastolic BP at Week 12 (mmHg)

DERIVED VARIABLES
-----------------
hscrp_pct_change    Percent change in hs-CRP from baseline to Week 12
clinical_response   Binary response (1 if ≥20% reduction in hs-CRP, else 0)

STUDY CONDUCT
-------------
adherence_pct       Treatment adherence (% of doses taken)
any_adverse_event   Any adverse event reported (0=No, 1=Yes)
serious_adverse_event Serious adverse event reported (0=No, 1=Yes)

NOTES:
- Missing values indicate patient dropout or missed visit
- hs-CRP is the primary inflammatory biomarker of interest
- Normal hs-CRP is typically <1 mg/L; 1-3 mg/L indicates moderate risk
