
PRECLINICAL ANXIOLYTIC STUDY - DATA DICTIONARY
================================================

STUDY OVERVIEW:
Evaluation of NeuroCalmX, a novel anxiolytic compound, in C57BL/6 mice 
using validated behavioral tests and physiological biomarkers.

Goal: Assess anxiolytic efficacy and determine effective dose range.

ANIMAL IDENTIFIERS & DESIGN
----------------------------
animal_id               Unique animal identifier (AN001-AN080)
sex                     Animal sex ("Male" or "Female")
dose_mgkg               NeuroCalmX dose in mg/kg (0, 5, 10, 20)
dose_group              Dose category ("Vehicle", "Low", "Medium", "High")
treatment_code          Binary treatment indicator (0=Vehicle, 1=Active)
cage                    Cage identifier (for mixed-model analysis)
body_weight_g           Body weight at study start (grams)
age_weeks               Age at study start (weeks)
baseline_anxiety_pct    Pre-treatment anxiety measure (% closed arm time)

ELEVATED PLUS MAZE (EPM) - Gold standard anxiety test
------------------------------------------------------
epm_open_time_pct       % time spent in open arms (higher = less anxious)
epm_open_entries        Number of entries into open arms
epm_closed_entries      Number of entries into closed arms
epm_total_distance_cm   Total distance traveled (locomotor activity)

OPEN FIELD TEST (OFT) - Exploration and anxiety
------------------------------------------------
oft_center_time_sec     Time in center zone (higher = less anxious)
oft_total_distance_cm   Total distance traveled
oft_rearing_count       Number of rearing events (exploration)
oft_grooming_sec        Time spent grooming (displacement behavior)

LIGHT-DARK BOX (LDB) - Light aversion test
-------------------------------------------
ldb_light_time_sec      Time in light compartment (higher = less anxious)
ldb_transitions         Number of crossings between compartments
ldb_latency_to_light_sec Time to first entry into light (lower = less anxious)

FORCED SWIM TEST (FST) - Depression-related behavior
-----------------------------------------------------
fst_immobility_sec      Time immobile (higher = more "depressed")
fst_swimming_sec        Time actively swimming
fst_climbing_sec        Time climbing walls

PHYSIOLOGICAL MEASURES
-----------------------
cort_baseline_ngml      Baseline plasma corticosterone (ng/mL)
cort_post_stress_ngml   Post-stress corticosterone
cort_delta_ngml         Change in corticosterone (stress response)
hrv_baseline_ms         Baseline heart rate variability (RMSSD, ms)
hrv_post_stress_ms      Post-stress HRV
temp_baseline_c         Baseline body temperature (°C)
temp_post_stress_c      Post-stress body temperature
food_intake_24h_g       24-hour food intake post-treatment (grams)

DERIVED MEASURES
-----------------
anxiety_index           Composite anxiety score (0-100, higher = more anxious)
treatment_response_pct  % change from baseline anxiety

NOTES:
- 80 animals total (10 per group, balanced by sex)
- Consider sex as covariate or stratification factor
- Cage effects present (use mixed models)
- Multiple outcomes allow multiverse analysis
- Dose-response analysis possible (linear trend, ANOVA)
